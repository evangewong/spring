Overview

We sell posters with memes on 'em.

Our posters are made of the finest environmentally-friendly material (recycled, of course) and centruple (that's 100 times) laminated to ensure quality.
An added layer of protective coat is also placed over the poster for more durability and protection.
In fact, we can say with upmost confidence that our products can serve as bullet-proof shields.
